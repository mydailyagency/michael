Flexible 3-day weight training program designed for your schedule with home and gym options.

  • Overhead Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps

Cardio Plan

Strategic 2-day cardio plan combining HIIT efficiency with LISS sustainability for optimal fat loss.

HIIT Session (Day 1)

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes light cardio
  • Work Interval: 30 seconds maximum effort
  • Rest Interval: 60 seconds recovery
  • Repeat: 8-12 rounds
  • Cool-down: 5 minutes light cardio
  • Total Time: 20-25 minutes

Exercise Options: Sprints, bike sprints, rowing sprints, battle ropes

LISS Session (Day 2)

  • Activity: Steady-state cardio
  • Intensity: 60-70% max heart rate
  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Pace: Able to hold conversation
  • Options: Walking, cycling, elliptical, stair climber

Cardio Guidelines:

  • HIIT Benefits: Time-efficient, metabolic boost, muscle preservation
  • LISS Benefits: Sustainable, recovery-friendly, fat oxidation
  • Scheduling: Can be done on any 2 days, separate from lifting
  • Progression: Increase HIIT rounds or LISS duration weekly

Meal Timing Strategy

Optimized meal timing for night shift work to align with your circadian rhythm and maximize fat loss.

Pre-Shift Meal (6-7 PM)

  • 40g protein
  • 60g complex carbs
  • 15g healthy fats
  • Example: Chicken breast, brown rice, vegetables

During Shift Snack (10-11 PM)

  • 20g protein
  • 15g carbs
  • 10g fats
  • Example: Greek yogurt, almonds, berries

Post-Shift Meal (6-7 AM)

  • 40g protein
  • 30g carbs
  • 15g fats
  • Example: Protein shake, eggs, avocado

Sleep Time Meal (1-2 PM)

  • 30g protein
  • 20g carbs
  • 10g fats
  • Example: Cottage cheese, fruit, nuts

Night Shift Nutrition Guidelines:

  • Front-load calories: 70% of daily protein before midnight
  • Avoid late-night eating: Minimize food between midnight-6 AM
  • Hydration: 1 gallon water throughout shift
  • Caffeine timing: Limit to first half of shift
  • Circadian alignment: Maintain consistent meal times
