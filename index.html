Hydration Protocol

Current: 0.8-1 gallon daily

Days 1-7 (August 12-18)

Target: 1 gallon daily

Instructions: Maintain consistent hydration throughout the day. Drink water evenly spaced, aiming for 8-10 glasses. Monitor urine color to ensure it's pale yellow.

Timing: Sip water throughout your shift, with a focus on consuming at least 16 oz upon waking and before each meal.

Days 8-10 (August 19-21)

Target: 1.5 gallons daily

Instructions: Increase water intake significantly. Carry a large water bottle and set reminders to drink every 30 minutes. This helps flush out sodium and reduce water retention.

Timing: Start your day with 20 oz of water immediately upon waking. Consume 16 oz every hour during your shift, and finish with 20 oz before bed.

Day 11 (August 22)

Target: 0.5 gallon (first half of day only)

Instructions: Drink most of your water in the morning and early afternoon. Stop drinking water by 3-4 PM to allow your body to shed excess water overnight. This helps achieve a drier, more defined look.

Timing: Consume 16 oz upon waking, 16 oz with your first meal, and 8 oz with your second meal. No water after 4 PM.

Day 12 (August 23)

Target: Sip as needed

Instructions: Only sip water when thirsty. Focus on appearance rather than hydration. Avoid large amounts of water to prevent bloating. Use small sips to stay comfortable without retaining water.

Timing: Limit intake to small sips (2-4 oz) only when absolutely necessary. Avoid drinking water 2-3 hours before the wedding event.

Recovery Recommendations for Night Shift Workers

Sleep Optimization
Nutrition Timing
Supplement Adjustments
Additional Recovery

Sleep Optimization

  • Maintain consistent 8am-4pm sleep schedule
  • Create a completely dark sleep environment (blackout curtains, eye mask)
  • Keep bedroom temperature cool (65-68F)
  • Use white noise to block daytime sounds
  • Avoid screens for 1 hour before bed

Nutrition Timing

  • First meal: Upon waking (around 4-5pm)
  • Second meal: Around 11pm-midnight (during shift)
  • Snack: Post-shift (around 7am) if needed
  • Avoid eating during sleep hours (8am-4pm)

Supplement Adjustments

  • Continue creatine (5g daily)
  • Continue whey protein (as needed to meet protein targets)
  • Consider reducing preworkout/energy drinks in the final 3 days to minimize water retention
  • Continue multivitamin
  • Add electrolyte supplementation during lower sodium phases

Additional Recovery Strategies

  • Daily stretching focusing on hip flexors and lower back
  • Foam rolling 3-4 times per week
  • Contrast showers (hot/cold) after training sessions
  • Consider 10-minute meditation sessions before bed to improve sleep quality

Exercise Modifications for Back/Hip Issues

Replace/Modify

  • Barbell squats Hack squats, leg press, or split squats (which he's already doing)
  • Deadlifts Romanian deadlifts with lighter weight, glute bridges, or hip thrusts
  • Any exercise causing pain Alternative that doesn't aggravate the area

Focus on

  • Core strengthening exercises that don't strain the lower back (planks, bird-dog)
  • Hip mobility work (dynamic stretching, foam rolling)
  • Maintaining proper form on all exercises
